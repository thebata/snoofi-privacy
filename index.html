Snoofi Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Snoofi ("the App") is an AI-powered dog caption app that uses your device camera to capture photos of dogs and generate humorous captions. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

  • Photos: Images you capture or select within the app are sent to AI services for caption generation. Photos are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save a creation.
  • Account Information: If you sign in, we collect your email address and display name via Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In).
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics data (e.g., feature usage, app crashes) through Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to improve the app experience.
  • Purchase History: Records of in-app purchases are stored to credit your account with the correct amount of treats.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered captions from your dog photos
  • To save your creations and preferences
  • To process in-app purchases
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Storage, Analytics, Crashlytics
  • Google Gemini / OpenAI — AI image analysis and caption generation
  • Google Play Billing — In-app purchase processing

Camera Permission

Snoofi requests access to your device camera solely to capture photos of dogs for caption generation. Camera access is only used when you actively take a photo within the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide app functionality.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • You can delete your account and all associated data from the app's Settings screen.
  • Saved creations can be individually deleted from your collection.

Children's Privacy

Snoofi is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
